A little about me

I’m Christine! I live in Austin, TX and currentely immersing myself into the world of design. I’m eager to learn, create, and innovate with people to develop valuable experiences.

My interest lies with the power art has on humanity and the way it intertwines with culture to leave lasting impressions. I was introduced to design when I took my first humanities class in high school and developed an eye for form, function, and aesthetics in architecture and art. My interest in art also includes music, culinary, and martial arts. I appreciate the way art is expressed through different mediums and am always looking for inspiration.

I started my career in risk consulting which was a great experience to connect with others to solve client problems. My job was to speak to and gather data from clients, analyze the data, and provide solutions for their concerns. Although, I enjoyed working with clients to understand their problems, I felt frustrated that I only provided solutions, but did not have the tools to create the solutions. I transitioned to a technology start-up as a Program Manager where I learned how to work cross-functionally in a fast-paced environment. This brought me closer to my interests in the art of technology, but I was still lacking the ability to create something. It wasn't until I discovered designing a good user experience, that I could see how cohesive technology, art, and design worked together. I saw the potential growth and was inspired by this new art form.

Whether it is creating a pleasing layout or an intuitive form that can save time, I found myself excited to finally create solutions to problems that were meaningful and had intention. With many years of experience both in the business and the tech world, I can bring my ability to work with clients and understanding of technology to use design as a way to create value to people.
